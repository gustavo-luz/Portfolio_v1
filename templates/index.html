About Me

I am a Production Engineering Student with a project and risk management background at different contexts. I always loved life hacks and that is why i like to optimize processes and activities. Since i discovered the IoT universe i became fascinated about it and started automating all the stuff i could.

I work with raspberry pi, esp32, arduinos, sensors, motors and recently started 3d printing as a hobby. As a software developer, my favorite language is python but i have worked with C++, R, Matlab and recently decided to learn JS, CSS and HTML. I have some experience with flask (e.g this website) and PostgreSQL but i am eager to learn Django and NoSQL databases. For my future projects, i want to work with computer vision, neural networks, deep learning, cloud services and telegram bots integrated with alexa.
